About Me

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Einstein Rock Mechanics Lab (MIT CEE). My work focuses on electric fracturing of rock for sustainable subsurface engineering. I am broadly interested in geothermal energy, fracture mechanics, and multiphysics numerical modeling.
